How to earn money online at home in this lockdown ?

In this modern time of the world, there is a lot of ways to earn money online if you have skills passion to perform and if you know how to do smart work. I'm giving you some most proven ways to make money online even if you're home in this pandemic lockdown situation.

If you want to earn money without any efforts and skills then you are in the wrong place. the online world is not giving you the instant money but if you'll work smartly then your work will definitely pay you off.

Do's and Dont's

Be aware of

  • Most of the messages you get for a job with a huge amount of salary are fake.
  • Never pay for the job of any company because companies never ask for money for the interviews.
  • Rather than going for a job counselor, you can directly go for the walk-in-interview.

Try to do

  • Before searching for any jobs online or offline you must have some skills. If you don't have any skill then don't waste your time and money. Try to learn some new skills.
  • You should have the patience to make money online because you can not earn overnight. Stay calm and work smart.

Here are some proven ways to earn money online.

1) Start Content writing

Content writing is the best and easiest way to earn money online as we know that there are a huge number of blogging websites that post many blogs daily so they need a content writer for their websites. You can contact those companies through many freelancing websites like freelancer, Toptal, Fiverr, Upwork, Guru.com, and many more. You can write for any topic now if you are thinking that I don't know how to write then let me tell you that you just have to do some research to write on any topic. You have to trust yourself for this. Still, you think that you cant then just try for once.

2) Video Editing

If you know video editing then you can contact any Youtuber or any Tiktoker. Not every Youtuber or Tiktoker knows video editing and for this, you can charge. You can also go to the freelancing sites (mentioned on point 1) where you'll find a lot of work. For video editing, you need very few things like a laptop, a video editing software like: Adobe premiere pro Corel video studio Light worksFinal cut pro Sony Vegas movie studio Cinelerra

3) Online tutoring

In this pandemic situation, students are not going to school, colleges, or any tuition so most of the students are joining online coaching classes. If you are good at any subject and want to teach then you can join online teaching platforms like Udemy, Unacademy, Edureka, Simplilearn, etc. You don't need to give whole day for this. You can give 2 or 3 hours a day and you can do other work for the rest of the day.

4) Be a freelancer

Every person has some skills in which they are good at. You can do freelancing in many fields like website developer, android application developer, Content writer, Digital marketer, Graphics designer, etc. You can utilize these skills on many freelancing sites. On these freelancing sites many you'll get many projects on these skills. So work for them and get paid. Never take too many projects at a time and try to finish the work within the deadline. Before freelancing you should have some experiences or any projects which you've done before. If you don't have, then firstly do some projects and polish your skills.

5) Start blogging

If you know writing you can start your own blog. All you need is a domain name, website, and blogs. Just select a topic on which you'll write, then you can post this blog on your website. If you don't want to buy a domain or you don't know how to make a website then you can start your blogging on google blogger where you'll get a free sub-domain. After posting your blogs try to get traffic as much as possible by using SEO and use Google Adsense and affiliate marketing for making money.

6) Start a youtube channel

Starting a Youtube channel is a free and easy way to earn money you just have to be patient. If you have skills or anything which you want to share with the whole world then you can do this. After starting the channel and posting the video you'll start getting views. Now you can use Google AdSense to earn money. More Views you get more money you'll get.

7) Do internships

You can do paid work from home internships according to your skills. If you want to do internships in web development or any field then you can apply on internships sites like Internshala, letsintern, youthforwork, Twenty19. On these you'll find a lot of internships on multiple skills. You can contact those companies through these platforms and after joining the organization they'll pay for your work. If you'll ask about my point of view, I like Internshala because I worked with many companies as an intern and joined there through Internshala.
